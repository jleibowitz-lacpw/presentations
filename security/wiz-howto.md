# Wiz: Cloud Security Platform

---

## Before You Start

**We recommend exploring official resources alongside this presentation:**

- **Official Docs:** https://docs.wiz.io/
- **Wiz Academy:** https://academy.training.wiz.io/
- **Videos:** https://docs.wiz.io/academy
- **Release Notes:** https://docs.wiz.io/release-notes

> This document is not a replacement for official documentation. It's designed to help you get up to speed with Wiz for effective cloud security posture management.

---

## What is Wiz?

- **Cloud security posture management** platform
- Real-time visibility across your cloud infrastructure
- Identifies misconfigurations, vulnerabilities, and compliance gaps
- Supports AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes, and more

---

## Key Capabilities

- **Asset Inventory** - Complete visibility of cloud resources
- **Risk Prioritization** - Focus on what matters most
- **Compliance Monitoring** - Track regulatory requirements
- **Vulnerability Management** - Identify and remediate security issues
- **Container Security** - Secure Kubernetes and container registries

---

## Accessing Wiz

**In our setup, we use SSO:**

1. Go to https://myapplications.microsoft.com/
2. Click the Wiz button
3. You'll be automatically logged in (if you have an account)

---

## Getting Started

1. Connect your cloud accounts
2. Review the risk dashboard
3. Prioritize findings by severity and business impact
4. Create remediation workflows
5. Monitor continuous compliance

---

## Next Steps

- Explore the dashboard
- Review your top findings
- Engage with your security team
- Check Wiz Academy for training
